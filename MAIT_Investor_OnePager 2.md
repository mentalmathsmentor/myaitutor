# MAIT: Your AI Study Mate
## One-Page Investor Pitch Summary

### The Problem
Year 11 & 12 students in NSW are overwhelmed by the **HSC Mathematics Advanced** curriculum. Modern tutoring is either:
1.  **Expensive**: $60-$120/hr for human tutors.
2.  **Impersonal**: Generic AI (ChatGPT) often hallucinates math and lacks curriculum alignment.
3.  **Distracting**: Tools aren't designed for focused, deep-work study.

### The Solution: MAIT
MAIT is a **curriculum-aware AI agent** designed specifically for NSW high school students. It acts as a "Mate" that knows the syllabus as well as you do.

- **Curriculum-Locked**: Grounded in the official NESA syllabus via RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).
- **Privacy-First**: Runs a Local SLM (Small Language Model) on-device for primary interactions, ensuring student data stays private.
- **Cognitive Wellness**: Tracks "Fatigue Metrics" and "Mastery Scores" to prevent burnout and ensure effective learning.
- **Adaptive Pedagogy**: Uses Bloom's Taxonomy to guide students from "Understanding" to "Evaluation."

### The Pilot Program (2026)
We are currently seeking primary partner schools for our 2026 Pilot.
- **Cost**: **FREE** for the duration of the pilot.
- **Goals**: Validate student engagement, refine the fatigue-detection engine, and quantify mastery improvement.
- **Security**: fully compliant with Australian student data privacy standards.

### Why MAIT?
Unlike generic LLMs, MAIT is built for the **Australian education context**. It's not just a chatbot; it's a cognitive companion that evolves with the student.
